About Hungarian 365

Your daily companion for learning Hungarian

Welcome to Hungarian365.com!

My name is Viktória and I'm a certified Hungarian grammar and literature teacher. I speak Hungarian (it's my mother tongue), English and Dutch fluently and in my free time I learn Italian too. As you can see, I'm a language enthusiast but I have another passion as well: I've been creating online content for more than 15 years and I've self-published some e-books as well. Hungarian365.com is a place where I can do what I love the most: sharing my knowledge and experience.

Whether you’re a beginner or brushing up on your skills, Hungarian 365 offers tools like flashcards, graded readers, and cultural tips to keep you motivated.

Why 365?

Hungarian is a unique and beautiful and unique language with deep cultural roots. I believe that language acquisition is a process that can be incredibly enjoyable but it's important how you approach it. My method focuses on turning studying into a DAILY habit, while making sure that the learner has fun as well!
