About Hungarian 365

Your daily companion for learning Hungarian

Our Mission

Hungarian 365 was created to make learning Hungarian simple, consistent, and enjoyable. Every day, we share a new phrase with its English translation, along with resources to help you practice and improve.

Whether you’re a beginner or brushing up on your skills, Hungarian 365 offers tools like flashcards, graded readers, and cultural tips to keep you motivated.

Why Hungarian?

Hungarian is a unique and beautiful language with deep cultural roots. We believe that learning it opens doors to new friendships, travel experiences, and a richer understanding of Central Europe.
